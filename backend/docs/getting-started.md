# Getting Started with MixFade

Welcome to **MixFade** – your professional audio analysis and A/B comparison tool! 🎵

> 📋 **Running v0.9.8** — Check out the [Version 0.9.8 Release Notes](/help/Version/0.9.8-overview) to see what's new

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Installation
1. Download MixFade:
   - **Windows**: Download the `.exe` installer and run it — one-click install with desktop shortcut

2. Launch MixFade from your desktop or Start Menu

### First Steps

#### 1. **Load Your Audio Files**
- Drag & drop audio files directly onto **Deck A** or **Deck B**, or use `Ctrl+O` to open a file
- Supported formats: MP3, WAV, FLAC, AIFF, AAC, OGG, and more
- Files are processed entirely locally — your audio never leaves your device

#### 2. **Play & Compare**
- Press `Space` to play/pause the active deck
- Use the **A/B Switch** to crossfade between decks and compare sources in real time
- Press `1` or `2` to switch instantly between Track A and Track B
- Enable **Link** to sync playback across both decks simultaneously
- Enable **Loop** (next to the Link button) to loop a deck continuously

#### 3. **Analyse Your Audio**
Open the **Analysis** tab in the sidebar (Activity Bar on the left) to see live A/B data:
- **Levels** — LUFS, peak, and RMS comparison
- **Frequencies** — Bass, mid, and high energy comparison
- **Stereo** — Phase correlation and stereo width
- **Spectrogram** — Time-frequency content visualization

Each section is collapsible — click the header to expand or collapse it and focus on what matters.

## 🎛️ Core Features

### **Waveform Decks**
- Dual stereo waveform rendering for Deck A and Deck B
- Click anywhere on the waveform to jump to that position
- Per-deck volume, mute, link, and loop controls
- Timestamp display showing current position and total duration

### **A/B Analysis Panel**
- **Levels**: Real-time LUFS, peak, and RMS monitoring for both tracks
- **Frequencies**: Spectrum energy comparison across bass, mid, and high bands
- **Stereo**: Phase correlation and stereo width side-by-side
- **Spectrogram**: Live frequency-over-time visualization
- All four sections are **collapsible** — collapse what you don't need to eliminate scrolling

### **Visualizer**
- Click the **Visualizer** tab (monitor icon) in the Activity Bar to enter fullscreen visual mode
- Seed-driven audio-reactive visuals powered by Butterchurn — each seed produces a unique look
- **Roll New Seed** to randomize the visual preset
- **Save Current Seed** to bookmark a look you like — saved seeds persist across sessions
- Load any saved seed from the list; delete ones you no longer want
- Press `Escape` to exit the Visualizer and return to the main view

### **Crossfade Engine**
- Smooth A/B crossfade with configurable duration (0.5 – 10 seconds)
- Crossfade curve options: Linear, Equal Power, Logarithmic
- Linked playback mode keeps both decks in sync during crossfades

## 🔧 Settings & Preferences

Access settings through the **gear icon** in the Activity Bar:
- **Analysis update rate** — control meter refresh speed
- **Crossfade duration & curve** — tune the A/B transition behaviour
- **Deck color theme** — choose from multiple colour pairings for Deck A / Deck B
- **Version** — current build version displayed at the bottom of the Settings panel
